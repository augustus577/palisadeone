## HOW TO RECORD THESE

### Option A: Record Yourself (Best for LinkedIn)
- Use your phone, good lighting (face a window), clean background
- Horizontal for YouTube/LinkedIn, vertical for Instagram/TikTok
- Look at the camera, not the screen
- Don't read word-for-word — use the script as a guide, be natural
- Edit with CapCut (free) — add captions, cuts, b-roll

### Option B: AI Video Tools (No Camera Needed)
- **HeyGen** (heygen.com) — $24/mo, creates AI spokesperson videos
  - Paste the script, pick an avatar, it generates a video
  - Looks professional, good for explainers and ads
- **Synthesia** (synthesia.io) — $22/mo, same concept
  - Good for the explainer and industry-specific videos
- **InVideo AI** (invideo.io) — generates videos from text prompts
  - Good for the fear video with stock footage + text overlays

### Option C: Screen Recording + Voiceover
- Record your dashboard.html with a screen recorder (OBS is free)
- Do a voiceover reading the explainer script
- Edit in CapCut
- This is great for the "What you're paying for" video

### Recommended Approach
1. Videos 1-4: Use HeyGen or record yourself
2. Video 5 (LinkedIn personal story): MUST be you on camera — authenticity sells
3. Video 2 (fear video): Use InVideo AI or CapCut with text overlays + stock footage
