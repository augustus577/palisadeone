## TIPS FOR SENDING

1. **Send volume**: 20-30 personalized emails per day (not mass blast)
2. **Best times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM
3. **Personalize**: Always use their name, company name, and industry
4. **CAN-SPAM**: Include your physical address and unsubscribe option
5. **Track opens**: Use a tool like Mailtrack (free Chrome extension) or HubSpot free CRM
6. **Follow up**: 3-touch sequence (initial → 3 days → 7 days)
7. **LinkedIn first**: Connect on LinkedIn BEFORE emailing — warmer touch
