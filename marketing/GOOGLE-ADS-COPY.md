## GOOGLE ADS SETUP TIPS

1. **Start with Search campaigns only** (not Display — too broad for your budget)
2. **Location targeting**: Maryland + Pennsylvania only
3. **Budget**: $20-30/day ($600-900/mo)
4. **Bid strategy**: Maximize conversions
5. **Conversion tracking**: Set up in Google Analytics — track clicks to cyber-assessment.html and contact.html form submissions
6. **Negative keywords** (exclude these):
   ```
   free cybersecurity tools
   cybersecurity jobs
   cybersecurity degree
   cybersecurity course
   cybersecurity training
   cybersecurity salary
   how to hack
   ```
7. **Ad extensions**:
   - Sitelinks: Pricing, Platform, Free Assessment, Contact
   - Callout: 24/7 Monitoring, No Contracts, Flat Rate, Live In Days
   - Call: your phone number
   - Location: your business address
