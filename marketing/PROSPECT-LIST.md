## HOW TO FIND EMAIL ADDRESSES

For prospects where we only have phone/website:

1. **Visit their website** → look for Contact page, About page, or Staff page
2. **hunter.io** → enter domain (e.g., "salujamedical.com") → shows email pattern
3. **Common patterns to try:**
   - info@[domain]
   - contact@[domain]
   - [firstname]@[domain]
   - office@[domain]
   - admin@[domain]
4. **Call the office** → ask for the email of the office manager or person in charge of IT/technology decisions
5. **LinkedIn** → find the owner/manager → DM them directly (warmer than cold email)
