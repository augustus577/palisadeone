# PALISADE ONE — Target Industries & Prospecting Guide (MD & PA)

---

## TIER 1: HIGHEST CONVERSION (Regulated + Compliance Pain)

### Healthcare (HIPAA)
- Doctor's offices, dental practices, urgent care
- Physical therapy, behavioral health, home health
- Small hospitals, imaging centers, labs
- **Why they buy**: HIPAA fines up to $1.5M/year, OCR audits increasing
- **Search terms**: "[City] medical practice", "[City] dental office", "[City] urgent care"
- **Decision maker**: Office Manager, Practice Administrator, or the Doctor themselves

### Law Firms (Client Data + Ethics Rules)
- Small-to-mid firms (5-100 attorneys)
- Personal injury, criminal defense, family law, corporate law
- **Why they buy**: Bar ethics rules require data protection, client trust, malpractice risk
- **Search terms**: "[City] law firm", "[City] attorney"
- **Decision maker**: Managing Partner, Office Administrator

### Accounting / CPA Firms (IRS Pub 4557 + Tax Data)
- Local CPA firms, bookkeeping services, tax preparers
- **Why they buy**: IRS requires Written Information Security Plan, peak target during tax season
- **Search terms**: "[City] CPA firm", "[City] accountant", "[City] bookkeeper"
- **Decision maker**: Managing Partner, IT contact

### Government Contractors (CMMC / NIST 800-171)
- Defense subcontractors, IT services for government
- **Why they buy**: CMMC Level 2 now required, lose contracts without it
- **Search terms**: "[City] defense contractor", "[City] government contractor"
- **Decision maker**: CEO, Compliance Officer, IT Director
- **HUGE in MD** — tons of DoD contractors around Baltimore, Annapolis, Columbia, Fort Meade area

## TIER 2: HIGH VALUE (Data-Rich + Aware of Risk)

### Financial Services
- Independent financial advisors, wealth management, credit unions
- Insurance agencies, mortgage companies
- **Why they buy**: SEC/FINRA cybersecurity requirements, SOC 2 audits
- **Search terms**: "[City] financial advisor", "[City] insurance agency"

### Real Estate
- Brokerages, title companies, property management
- **Why they buy**: Wire fraud is massive in real estate ($400M+ lost in 2024), BEC attacks
- **Search terms**: "[City] real estate brokerage", "[City] title company"

### Construction / Trades
- General contractors, HVAC, plumbing, electrical companies with 20+ employees
- **Why they buy**: Ransomware targets companies that can't afford downtime
- **Search terms**: "[City] construction company", "[City] general contractor"

### Nonprofits
- Mid-size nonprofits with donor data
- **Why they buy**: Donor trust, grant requirements often mandate security controls
- **Search terms**: "[City] nonprofit organization"

## TIER 3: VOLUME PLAY (Every Business Needs This)

### Professional Services
- Marketing agencies, consulting firms, architecture firms, engineering firms
- Staffing agencies, recruiting firms

### Retail / E-commerce
- Local retail chains (5+ locations)
- E-commerce businesses with customer payment data (PCI)

### Education (Private)
- Private schools, tutoring centers, trade schools
- **Why they buy**: Student data (FERPA), parent trust

### Manufacturing
- Small-to-mid manufacturers, especially those with IP to protect
- Food production, pharmaceutical, auto parts

## HOW TO FIND SPECIFIC COMPANIES

### Google Search Queries
```
"dental practice" "Baltimore" site:yelp.com
"law firm" "Philadelphia" site:google.com/maps
"CPA firm" "Columbia MD"
"medical practice" "Annapolis" "contact"
"government contractor" "Maryland" "cybersecurity"
```

### LinkedIn Search
- Go to linkedin.com/search
- Filter by: Location → [City], Industry → [Industry], Company Size → 11-200
- Look for: Owner, CEO, Managing Partner, IT Director, Office Manager

### Google Maps
- Search: "medical offices near Baltimore MD"
- Click through results, get contact info from their websites
- Look for email on Contact page or use hunter.io to find emails

### Directories
- healthgrades.com (healthcare practices)
- avvo.com (law firms)
- upcounsel.com (law firms)
- sam.gov (government contractors — search by NAICS code + state)
- bbb.org (general businesses by category and location)

### Email Finding Tools
- hunter.io (free tier: 25 searches/month)
- apollo.io (free tier: decent for finding contacts)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($99/mo — worth it if serious about outreach)
