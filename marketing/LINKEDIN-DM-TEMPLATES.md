# PALISADE ONE — LinkedIn DM Outreach Templates
# Use from Augustus's personal LinkedIn profile

---

## STRATEGY: Connect First, Then Message

### Step 1: Send Connection Request WITH a note

**Connection Note (300 char max):**
```
Hi [First Name] — I run a cybersecurity company in the Mid-Atlantic area and I'm connecting with [industry] leaders in [Maryland/PA]. Would love to connect. — Augustus
```

**Alt version:**
```
Hey [First Name] — saw you're running [Company Name] in [City]. I help local businesses with cybersecurity. Always looking to connect with fellow business owners in the area.
```

### Step 2: After They Accept (wait 1 day)

---

## DM 1: Warm Opener (Day after connection)

```
Hey [First Name], thanks for connecting!

Quick question — does [Company Name] have cybersecurity monitoring in place right now? Things like endpoint protection, 24/7 monitoring, that kind of thing?

Asking because I run Palisade One — we provide managed cybersecurity for businesses in [MD/PA] and I'm always curious how local companies are handling it.

No pitch, genuinely curious.
```

---

## DM 2: If They Reply "No" or "Not Sure"

```
Honestly, that's super common. Most businesses under 500 employees don't — it's usually too expensive or too complicated to set up.

That's exactly why we built Palisade One. We put enterprise-grade protection on every device for a flat $399/month. AI-powered, 24/7 monitoring, the whole thing.

If you're curious where [Company Name] stands, we have a free 10-minute cyber risk assessment:

palisadeone.com/cyber-assessment.html

No obligation — it just shows your gaps across HIPAA, NIST, PCI, whatever applies to you. Worth a look?
```

---

## DM 3: If They Reply "Yes, we have something"

```
Nice — that's good to hear. Mind if I ask what you're using?

Reason I ask: a lot of businesses I talk to have antivirus or basic monitoring but don't have actual EDR (endpoint detection and response) or a SOC monitoring things 24/7. There's a big difference between "we have Norton" and "we have someone watching our network at 2 AM."

Not trying to sell you anything — just happy to give you an honest take on whether what you have is solid or if there are gaps.
```

---

## DM 4: The Free Assessment Push

```
Hey [First Name], one more thing and then I'll leave you alone —

We built a free cyber risk assessment tool that maps your security posture against NIST, HIPAA, PCI, and other frameworks. Takes about 10 minutes.

Even if you never use our services, it's genuinely useful to see where you stand. A lot of business owners are surprised by the results.

palisadeone.com/cyber-assessment.html

Let me know if you have any questions about it.
```

```
Hey [First Name], thanks for connecting.

I see you're handling IT for [Company Name] — respect. Doing IT for a [industry] company is no joke, especially with the threat landscape right now.

Curious if that's something you'd ever consider?
```
