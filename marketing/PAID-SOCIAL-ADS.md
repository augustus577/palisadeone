## AD CREATIVE TIPS

### Images (create in Canva — free)
- Use dark backgrounds (#03050A or #070C14)
- Teal (#00FFD1) for accent text and highlights
- White for headlines
- Keep text minimal — 5-7 words max on the image
- Include logo in corner
- Dimensions: 1080x1080 (square) for feed, 1080x1920 (vertical) for stories/reels

### Suggested Canva Templates to Search:
- "cybersecurity social media"
- "dark tech ad template"
- "business security ad"

### A/B Testing Plan:
- Run 2 ads per campaign for 5 days
- Kill the one with lower CTR
- Scale the winner
- Test new creative every 2 weeks
