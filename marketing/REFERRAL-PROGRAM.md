## CLIENT REFERRAL PROGRAM

### Structure:
- **Referrer gets**: $200 credit on their next month (or $200 cash/gift card)
- **Referred business gets**: First month free ($399-899 value)
- **You get**: A new client at zero acquisition cost

### How It Works:
1. Existing client refers a business
2. Referred business signs up for any tier
3. After their first paid month, the referrer gets their reward
4. No limit on referrals

### Launch Email to Clients:
```

palisadeone.com/cyber-assessment.html

Thanks for being a client. We appreciate you.
```

## STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS (Force Multiplier)

These people already talk to your ideal clients. Partner with them.

### 1. IT Companies / MSPs (Managed Service Providers)
**The pitch**: "You handle IT. We handle security. Let's partner."
- Many MSPs don't offer real security (no EDR, no SOC, no SIEM)
- They know their clients need it but can't provide it
- **Offer**: White-label or co-branded Palisade One for their clients
- **Revenue share**: 10-20% recurring commission on every client they bring
- **Target MSPs in MD/PA**: Baltimore Consulting, Bmore Technology, CMIT Solutions

### 2. Insurance Agents (Cyber Insurance)
**The pitch**: "Your clients need cybersecurity to get/keep cyber insurance. We provide it."
- Cyber insurance underwriters now REQUIRE EDR, MFA, monitoring
- Insurance agents need to help clients meet these requirements
- **Offer**: Palisade One = automatic cyber insurance compliance
- **Revenue share**: $50-100/month per client they refer
- **Target**: Independent insurance agents in MD/PA (see prospect list)

### 3. Accountants / CPAs
**The pitch**: "Your clients trust you with their data. Help them protect it."
- CPAs handle sensitive financial data for dozens of businesses
- They're trusted advisors — their recommendation carries weight
- **Offer**: Refer clients → earn $100/referral + get your own firm protected at a discount
- **Target**: Small CPA firms from prospect list

### 4. Lawyers
**The pitch**: "You handle compliance. We handle the technical controls."
- Business lawyers advising on contracts, compliance, data protection
- They can recommend Palisade One as part of their compliance advice
- **Offer**: Partner referral → $100/client or reciprocal referrals

### 5. Business Consultants / Fractional CTOs
**The pitch**: "Add cybersecurity to your service offering without building it."
- Consultants advising SMBs on operations, technology, growth
- Cybersecurity is always a gap they identify but can't fill
- **Offer**: White-label or referral commission
