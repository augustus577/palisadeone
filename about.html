Our Mission

THE PROBLEM
WE SOLVE

Small and mid-sized businesses are the most targeted — and least protected — organizations in the world. Attackers know that a 20-person accounting firm or a regional medical practice doesn't have the resources of a Fortune 500 company.

But they have just as much valuable data. Patient records. Financial documents. Client information. And most of them are running on basic antivirus software from 2015 while ransomware gangs use AI-powered tools to find them.

The enterprise security tools that could stop these attacks — SentinelOne, CrowdStrike, Darktrace — exist. But they're priced for enterprises. The platforms that are priced for SMBs don't actually work against modern attacks.

We closed that gap. Palisade One is enterprise-grade security, built from the ground up for businesses that can't afford a breach — and finally priced like they can't afford one either.

"The only difference between a protected business and a breached one is whether someone decided to take security seriously before the attack happened."
We own our entire stack
No licensing fees from CrowdStrike, S1, or ConnectWise passed to clients. We built it, we control it, you benefit.
AI is built in, not bolted on
Every alert, report, and recommendation runs through AI. Your team gets context and plain English — not 10,000 raw log lines.
We assume breach
Zero trust isn't a feature — it's our default posture. We design every deployment as if attackers are already inside.
